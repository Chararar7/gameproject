Assassin's Creed Valhalla is an action role-playing video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and published by Ubisoft. It was released in November 2020 for various platforms, including PlayStation, Xbox, and PC. The game is set in the 9th century during the Viking invasion of Britain and follows the story of Eivor, a Viking raider who becomes embroiled in the conflict between the Assassins and the Templars. The gameplay features exploration, combat, and stealth, as well as a settlement-building mechanic where players can manage resources and improve their Viking clan's home. The game has received critical acclaim for its immersive open-world, engaging storyline, and refined gameplay mechanics.
